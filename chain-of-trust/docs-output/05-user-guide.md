# Understanding VIVIM

## A Human's Guide to Sovereign AI Memory

---

## The Problem: You Don't Own Your AI Conversations

Every time you talk to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI assistant, you're creating something valuable. You're sharing your questions, your creative ideas, your work problems, your personal thoughts. You're essentially creating an external memory of your mind.

But here's the uncomfortable truth: **you don't own that memory**. The company that makes the AI does.

- They store your conversations on their servers
- They can read your conversations
- They can use your conversations to train their models
- They can share your conversations with others
- If you switch to a different AI, you lose everything
- If they change their policies, your data is affected

This is like renting your memories instead of owning them. VIVIM changes this.

---

## What is VIVIM?

VIVIM is a **sovereign vault**—a protected space on your own device where every AI conversation you ever have gets stored, encrypted, and controlled by you.

Think of it like a secure vault in your house. Inside:
- Every AI conversation you've ever had
- From every AI provider you've ever used
- Searchable, organized, portable
- Encrypted with keys only you possess

The vault is yours. The keys are yours. The data is yours.

---

## How Does It Work?

### 1. You Get a Vault

When you start with VIVIM, you create a **vault** on your device. This involves:
- Writing down 24 words (your mnemonic)
- These words generate your master key
- Your key never leaves your device
- Your data never leaves your device either

If you lose your 24 words, you lose your vault. This is by design—you have total control.

### 2. Conversations Come In

Every AI conversation you have gets captured and stored in your vault:
- **Browser extension**: Captures web AI chats automatically
- **Import**: Upload your history from ChatGPT, Claude, etc.
- **IDE plugins**: Capture coding AI conversations
- **Mobile**: Capture on-the-go conversations

Everything enters through the **Universal Stream**—a single, secure doorway that verifies each piece of data, encrypts it, and stores it properly.

### 3. Your Data is Organized

Inside the vault, your conversations become **ACUs** (Atomic Conversation Units). Each ACU is:
- Encrypted with its own unique key
- Tagged with ownership information
- Connected to other related ACUs through a knowledge graph
- Searchable by semantic meaning

You can search: "what did I ask about X last year?" and find it instantly—even across different AI providers.

### 4. You Control What Happens

Here's where VIVIM gets powerful. You decide:

**Private (Tier 0)**: Stays in your vault, never leaves
**Personal (Tier 1-2)**: You can share or export as you choose  
**Shared (Tier 3)**: Company or co-governed—needs dual approval to export/sell
**Regulated (Tier 5)**: Locked by legal requirements—never leaves

## The Co-Governance Model

This is one of VIVIM's most important innovations.

When you use AI at work, some of your conversations may involve company secrets, client information, or work product. Companies typically want total control over this. VIVIM offers a better way:

**You keep your memory**. Your vault, your device, your keys.

**Company gets governance** over specific scopes. Not access to your whole vault—just the ability to approve/reject exports of designated content.

**Dual control**. For company-confidential content, both you AND your company must approve any export or sale.

**Precision**. Not your entire life becomes co-governed—just the specific parts that involve the company.

**Portability**. When you leave, you keep your memory. The work-related parts stay tagged and governed, but your cognitive continuity is yours.

This is the first system that honors both human sovereignty AND legitimate institutional rights—at the same time.

## The Detection System

VIVIM doesn't just store your data. It also watches for unauthorized use.

The **Sentinel** system includes 13 detection algorithms that can:

- **Membership Inference**: Detect if your data was likely used in training
- **Canary Tokens**: Plant unique phrases that reveal if training occurred
- **Cross-Provider Detection**: See if data given to one provider appeared in another
- **Evidence Generation**: Create cryptographic proof for legal action

You can see a dashboard of your "exposure"—which models likely contain your data, which canaries were triggered, which providers appear trustworthy.

This transforms privacy from "hope and trust" to "measurable and verifiable."

## The Marketplace

Your AI memory is valuable. VIVIM lets you capture that value.

On the marketplace, you can:
- **List** specific conversations or datasets for sale
- **Set terms**: who can buy, what they can do with it, for how long, at what price
- **Earn money**: receive payment directly for your data
- **Maintain control**: revoke access anytime

Buyers (researchers, AI companies, etc.) can purchase data with:
- **ZK proofs**: verify properties without seeing content
- **Minimum disclosure**: get only what's needed for their purpose
- **Encrypted delivery**: data comes in sealed packages

For co-governed data, revenue splits automatically between you and the company per your contract.

## Common Questions

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. Encryption happens on your device with keys that never leave. The vault file is indistinguishable from random data without your key.

**What if I lose my 24 words?**
Your vault is gone. This is intentional—no backdoor means no one else can access your data either. Write down your words, store them safely.

**Can VIVIM read my data?**
No. VIVIM is local software. There's no server that could read your data. The encryption keys exist only in your device's memory.

**What happens if my device is stolen?**
The vault is encrypted at rest. Without your passphrase/mnemonic, the data is unreadable. For extra security, you can bind keys to hardware (TPM/Secure Enclave).

**Can I switch providers?**
Absolutely. Your vault is independent of any AI provider. You can use Claude, then ChatGPT, then a new AI—all while maintaining the same memory.

**Can my employer access my vault?**
No. Unless you explicitly share specific content through the marketplace or sharing system, your employer has no access. Co-governance means they can approve/reject exports of their content—they can't reach into your vault.

## The Big Picture

VIVIM is building the missing layer in the AI ecosystem: **human ownership**.

Currently, AI companies own the memory of your interactions. You rent space in their products. If you leave, you lose everything.

VIVIM reverses this. You own your memory. AI companies must negotiate for access. You can choose what's private, what's shared, what's sold. You can detect if your data was used without permission. You can take your memory with you.

This isn't just a product. It's a fundamental shift in the relationship between humans and AI—making the human the center, not the AI company.
