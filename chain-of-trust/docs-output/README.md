## Key Themes

1. **Human Sovereignty**: User owns their data, controls keys, decides what happens
2. **Co-Governance**: Companies get governance without custody—shared control, not total control  
3. **Detection & Evidence**: Privacy becomes measurable, not just promised
4. **Marketplace**: Data becomes an asset users can monetize on their terms
5. **Biological Architecture**: Following nature's proven protection systems
