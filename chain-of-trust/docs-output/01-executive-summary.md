# VIVIM: The Sovereign Memory Protocol

## Executive Summary

VIVIM is a revolutionary protocol that gives humans complete ownership and control over their AI conversation history. It creates a unified, encrypted vault that consolidates every AI interaction across all providers—ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, local models, and future assistants—into a single sovereign archive that belongs entirely to the user.

This is not a product feature. It is a fundamental shift in the relationship between humans and AI. Currently, AI companies own the memory of every conversation. VIVIM reverses this: the human owns the memory, and AI companies must negotiate for access.

---

## The Problem

**Every AI conversation creates permanent value**—accumulated learning, judgment patterns, creative approaches, professional knowledge, and intimate thoughts. Today, that value is captured entirely by the platforms.

- Your conversations exist only in their products
- You cannot search across providers
- You cannot move your memory to new tools
- You cannot decide who can use your data
- You cannot detect if your data was used without permission
- You cannot monetize your own intellectual history

This creates a world where humans are perpetual tenants in their own cognitive infrastructure.

---

## The VIVIM Solution

VIVIM creates a **human-owned sovereign vault** that sits on your device, encrypts everything with keys only you possess, and gives you complete control over what stays private, what gets shared, and what can be sold.

### Core Capabilities

**1. Unified Memory Archive**
- Consolidates every AI conversation across all providers
- Each conversation becomes an Atomic Conversation Unit (ACU)
- Encrypted at rest with per-ACU keys
- No provider has access to the raw data

**2. Cross-Provider Search & Context**
- Semantic search across your entire intellectual history
- 8-layer context assembly for AI assistants
- Knowledge graph connecting related ideas across providers
- Your AI assistant remembers everything you've ever discussed

**3. Granular Rights Management**
- Every conversation tagged with ownership tiers (T0-T5)
- Personal vs. work vs. regulated content automatically classified
- Third-party governance for shared data (co-governance model)
- Precise span-level segmentation within conversations

**4. Sovereign Detection**
- 13 detection algorithms verify if your data was used without permission
- Membership inference, canary tokens, holographic watermarks
- Evidence packages for legal action
- Collective Sovereignty Index showing provider behavior

**5. Data Marketplace**
- Sell your data on your terms
- Minimum-disclosure review packets for buyers
- Revenue sharing with co-governing parties
- Zero-knowledge proofs of data properties

## The Co-Governance Model

VIVIM solves a previously impossible problem: **human custody with shared governance**.

When you use AI at work, your conversations may contain company-confidential information. Today, companies demand total control over your tools. VIVIM instead allows:

- **You keep full custody** of your memory at all times
- **Company gets governance** over designated scopes
- **Dual-control required** for exports/sales of covered material
- **Precision**: Only affected parts are governed, not your entire life
- **Portability**: You keep your cognitive continuity when you leave

This creates a mature relationship where both human sovereignty and institutional rights are honored.

## Technical Specifications

### Encryption
- **Master Key**: Derived from 24-word BIP-39 mnemonic via Argon2id
- **Vault Encryption**: SQLCipher with AES-256-CBC
- **Per-ACU Encryption**: XChaCha20-Poly1305 with unique keys
- **Key Hierarchy**: HKDF-derived keys for each purpose

### Storage
- **ACU Format**: Encrypted, signed, individually addressable
- **Merkle Tree**: Integrity verification for all content
- **Vector Index**: Semantic search via HNSW
- **Knowledge Graph**: Cross-provider semantic edges

### Blockchain
- **Consent Registry**: On-chain consent commitments
- **Marketplace**: ZK-rollup for transactions
- **Proof of Sovereignty**: Validator weighting by sovereignty contribution
